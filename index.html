Live pricing. "Current (Live)" is fetched through the bundled /api/quotes serverless function (proxying Yahoo Finance) on every page load and Refresh. "Close @ Txn" is the fixed official closing price on the transaction date (or nearest prior trading day). If the API is unreachable, the last-known close from 14 May 2026 is shown and the indicator turns amber.

Reading the Change % — purchases vs. sales differ. For a purchase, Change % is the unrealised gain/loss on the position since it was bought (higher = good). For a sale, Change % is what the security did after the exit: a positive number means the price rose after selling (gains forgone); a negative number means the price fell after selling (a loss avoided). The two tabs are kept separate because mixing them would be misleading.
